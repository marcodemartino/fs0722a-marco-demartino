Focaccia with caramelized onions,Cranberies,and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranbberies, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

image:Sally vegas

featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (35og) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

for the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
    (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it,s not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranbberies

Method

1.Make the focaccia dough

in a medium-sized mixing boel, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough infredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the dough, thoug i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
